About my Trip

This photo I took outside of the National Library of Ireland in Dublin. I took an Irish History course and this was when our class visited a Yates exhibit. Yates was a very famous Irish Poet who has a Nobel Prize in Literature and is the first Irish person to accomplish that feat.

This is a photo of me in the city centre of Ireland next to the River Liffy.You cannot go swimming in the water as it goes directly into the bay and has a lot of sewage going into it. This river was used in trading and for connecting Dublin to other countries by sea.
